Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a video call?

To start a video call, navigate to the Video Call page and click on "Start Call." Ensure your webcam and microphone permissions are enabled.

What if my video or audio isn't working?

Ensure your device has given permissions to access the microphone and camera. Also, check that your browser supports WebRTC.

Can I start an audio call instead of video?

Yes, navigate to the Audio Call page if available, where only the microphone will be used, without accessing the camera.

How do I end a call?

Simply click the "End Call" button on the Video or Audio Call page, and the call will be terminated immediately.
